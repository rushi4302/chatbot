Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, reason, and make decisions like humans do. AI works by using algorithms and machine learning techniques to process data and identify patterns, which can then be used to make predictions or take actions based on those patterns. For example, an AI system might use natural language processing to understand human speech and generate responses, or it might use image recognition to recognize objects in a photograph.
